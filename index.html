About Us

VERITY CHRISTIAN FAITH MINISTRY is an organization of likeminded individuals and groups, who have firmly and solely resolved to unite together resources, efforts, skills etc for the collective growth of the Christian faith, unflinching support for the teaching of true doctrine, and for the practice and fellowship of Christianity, to individuals, groups, institutions, the society, and humanity at large, with the goal of promotion and perfection of Christian faith, to the glory of God and to the blessing of humanity.

It is our firm belief and conviction that through this platform, where likeminded individuals, groups, institutions can band together and jointly harmonize resources, we can unite for the propagation of the true teachings and practices of Christian faith and belief, as taught, exemplified, practiced and directed in the scriptures.

VISION

To be an organisation of high standing for the promotion, support, and perfection of Christian faith, in doctrine, fellowship and all faith related works, to the glory of God and the blessing of humanity.

MISSION

To serve as a strong platform which provides advocacy, consultancy, Information Technology services, amongst others, to contribute, encourage, and promote the growth of fellowship, unity and perfection of the Christian faith globally.

OBJECTIVES

  1. A platform to aid the ministers and pastors in basic administration duties and activities of the church and their members.
  2. A platform for evangelism and proclamation of the truth and teachings of the Bible.
  3. A platform for speedy dissemination of information across all branches of the Church(es), a unit Church or congregation and its members.
  4. A platform for the welfare of members and individuals, hence promoting fellowship, love and brotherly kindness.
  5. A platform to keep track of the spread of churches, congregations, denominations and Christianity in the world or in any geographical location, and reach of the gospel in the world.

FEATURES

VERITY offers the following services/features to individuals and churches:

  1. Church Administration
  2. Social Media Network
  3. Registry & Events creation,sharing, locating and tracking (for members, churches and congregations)
  4. Devotionals/Online store
  5. Bible Research & Study (Topics, materials, discussions etc)
  6. Welfare Ministry (www.helpingalms.com)

01 Church Administration

This is a Church management solution for effective management of membership data, member communication, church-to-member communication, events creation, sharing and tracking, attendance and reports collection and keeping, centralized church data for easy access and storage.

02 Social Media Network

A platform for social interaction amongst members, with the Church, and connecting with Christians within your vicinity, and Christians beyond.

03 Registry & Events-sharing

A registry of all Churches locally and globally, their addresses, directions, contact persons to reach in cases of emergencies, and brief information of the congregation, its membership, and the ministries etcetera, with events creation, sharing and tracking is possible, timely delivery of invitations to events for churches, branches and members.

04 Devotionals/Online store

A platform for social interaction amongst members, with the Church, and connecting with Christians within your vicinity, and Christians beyond.

05 Biblical Research & Study

A search engine, encyclopaedia and research website for research and Bible-based answers on biblical questions, biblical knowledge and information from scholars.

06 Welfare ministry

An online platform where fellowship and love can be encouraged as Christians have the opportunity to join hands in granting the request/wish of another, contribute to a good cause, empathise with a brother/sister in their difficulty and render aid, contribute to a project etcetera.
